About Me

Design that speaks before words do.

I'm Alif Hossan, a dedicated logo and brand identity designer based in Bangladesh, working with clients worldwide. With over 4 years of professional experience, I bring brands to life through purposeful, memorable marks.

My design process is rooted in deep brand research and visual storytelling — I don't just design logos, I engineer first impressions. From minimalist wordmarks to complex emblems, every decision is intentional.

I believe a great logo is your brand's first — and loudest — handshake with the world.
