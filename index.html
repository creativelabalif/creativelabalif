About Me

Design that speaks before words do.

I'm Alif Hossan, a dedicated logo and brand identity designer with over 4 years of professional experience. I work with startups, small businesses, and established brands across Bangladesh and internationally.

My process is rooted in research and storytelling — every mark I create is built to carry the soul of a brand. From wordmarks to emblems, I design with purpose, precision, and a keen eye for timeless aesthetics.

I believe a great logo is more than a symbol — it's the first handshake between a brand and its audience.
